"A jack of all trades and a master of none, is oftentimes better than a master of one."

Justin Rodriguez is a multidisciplinary creative who executes ideas across artistic and entrepreneurial domains. As the founder of streetwear brand Coursework and adult bib company Dripbib, he brings a unique perspective that blends art, design, and business.

Specializing in character-driven illustration and graphic design, his work is focused on sparking joy and inspiring creativity. Whether it's a brand identity, an illustration series, or a product design, every project is approached with the same passion for craft and storytelling.
